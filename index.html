About
I ♥ BETHNAL GREEN

I ♥ BETHNAL GREEN is the online home of SAVE OUR SAFER STREETS, a group of Bethnal Green residents who want to keep and improve the newly built pedestrian-friendly streets around Old Bethnal Green Road, Columbia Road and Arnold Circus.

Our much-loved new, safe streets and community spaces are being threatened with removal by Tower Hamlets Council, who have launched a consultation to take out the £2 million of changes which were implemented in 2021.

Beyond the immediate campaign to save our new streets, parklets and community spaces, we will encourage the council to engage with all the residents in Bethnal Green to explore ways to make our public spaces better. Everyone wants to improve air quality and create safer streets for people to walk, talk and play on. We believe this is an ongoing experiment that we want everyone to feel they can benefit from.

ACTION

Complete the Tower Hamlets Council survey by 31 July!

There are two surveys to fill in - one for Weavers and the other for Old Bethnal Green Road.

If you are local to the area, make sure you use your code. It's in the consultation pack which was posted to you. This code can be used by each member of your household if they fill in the survey online.

If you want to stop the new seats, parklets and community spaces being taken out, then tick “oppose” to all the proposed removals.

There is also space for leaving comments so you can make suggestions for how the streets could work better for the whole community!

The only way we are going to build a cleaner and safer environment in Bethnal Green is to show how many of us want this. Please take this one action!

In addition, please consider writing to the Tower Hamlets Mayor and your local councillors. Also let your friends and neighbours know what is going on and, if they want to keep and improve the new green spaces and safer streets in Bethnal Green, ask them to complete the consultation too.
