A judge has granted us permission to appeal the Dec 17 judicial review ruling. We will now need to raise another £20,000 to fund it. Please donate to our Crowdjustice fund if you're able. We have got this far thanks to the more than 1,200 people who have thrown in whatever they can, and we hugely appreciate every single donation.

New transport strategy consultation

Tower Hamlets council is consulting on a new transport strategy which removes or waters down several policies that supported safer, healthier, friendlier streets. This strategy will shape council transport policy for years to come. Please respond to the consultation by 28th April 2025. It will only take you a few minutes and we've written a handy guide.

We did not win the court case

On Dec 17 2024, the High Court ruled that the Mayor of Tower Hamlets' decision to remove Low Traffic Neighbourhoods in Bethnal Green was legal. Following months of hard work and a truly moving show of support from our community, we are deeply disappointed that this judge has not found in our favour.
